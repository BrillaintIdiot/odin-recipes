Lasagna

World's Best Lasagna Recipe

This lasagna recipe takes a little work, but it is so satisfying and filling that it's worth it!

Ingredients

  • Sausage
  • Ground Beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomatoes

Steps

  1. Gather ingredients
  2. Cook all the meat with onion and garlic
  3. Stir in remaining ingredients
  4. Layer pasta and fillings
  5. Top with cheese
  6. Bake in the oven
  7. Let cool
  8. Dig in
