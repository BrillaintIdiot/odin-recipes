Butternut Squash Soup Recipe

Great tasting soup for soup hater

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Potatoes
  • Onion
  • Celery
  • Butternut

Steps

  1. Gather ingredients
  2. Melt butter and cook vegetables until browned
  3. Boil over medium heat for 40 minutes
  4. Blend contents
  5. Enjoy your soup
