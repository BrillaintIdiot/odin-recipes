Beef Stew

World's Best Lasagna Recipe

A warm hearty stew for cold nights

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Beef chuck
  • Potatoes
  • Mushrooms
  • Onions

Steps

  1. Gather ingredients
  2. Cook all the meat in instant pot
  3. Stir in remaining ingredients
  4. Close lid and let the pressure cook prepare the stew
